Title : Success Way "Textured Dial" Collection



The Success Way collection is a series of modern, high-fashion timepieces that blend a classic integrated bracelet design with a unique "hammered" or "honeycomb" textured dial. These watches are perfect for those who want a luxury look (reminiscent of the Audemars Piguet Royal Oak or Tissot PRX) at an affordable price point.



Specifications :
• Brand: Success Way
• Movement: Quartz (Battery powered) for precise timekeeping.
• Dial: High-definition 3D textured dial with applied hour markers.
• Case Material: Stainless Steel/Alloy with a brushed and polished finish.
• Water Resistance: 30M Water Resist (Suitable for hand washing and light rain; not for swimming).
• Functions: Analog time display with an automatic Date window at the 3 o’clock position.
• Crystal: Scratch-resistant Mineral Glass.


Size & Fit (Standard Men's Size)
• Case Diameter: Approx. 40mm - 42mm (Standard size for most wrists).
• Band Width: 20mm - 22mm (Integrated style).
